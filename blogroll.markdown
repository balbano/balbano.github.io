In my continued effort to unearth the traditions of the internet of yore, I present to you: my blogroll! This is a mix of stuff that I actively read and stuff that I'm intending to read and stuff that I read a really good post on one time, added to this list, and then forgot about. These are mostly, but not entirely, about TTRPGS.

This list could always be longer! Email me something you think I'd like about games, clown, or pumping iron! [brendan@brendanalbano.com](mailto:brendan@brendanalbano.com)

## Blogs and Games Press

- [Traverse Fantasy](https://traversefantasy.blogspot.com/): You never know if you're gonna get RPG theory or political theory, and they're both good.
- [Bastionland](https://www.bastionland.com/): Into the Odd is brilliant and Chris McDowall's thoughts on games are interesting.
- [Rise Up Comus](http://riseupcomus.blogspot.com/): A great blog, and I keep meaning to read the [Designing Dungeons](https://dungeons.hismajestytheworm.games/) course.
- [Goblin Punch](https://goblinpunch.blogspot.com/): I regularly reread the [Dungeon Checklist](https://goblinpunch.blogspot.com/2016/01/dungeon-checklist.html).
- [Rascal](https://www.rascal.news/): Worker-owned TTRPG journalism!
- [Mothership.blog](https://www.mothership.blog/): Worker-owned videogame journalism!
- [Looking at Picture Books](https://lookingatpicturebooks.com/): in particular the one about [Goodnight Moon](https://lookingatpicturebooks.com/p/goodnight-moon)

## Magazines / Blog Anthologies

- [Knock!](https://www.themerrymushmen.com/product/knock-1-tmm/): Go wild and get all five issues!
- [Glaive](https://store.hotheadcollective.com/): I got issue #2 at Dark Future and it ruled.
- [Prismatic Wisdom](https://www.prismaticwasteland.com/shop/p/prismatic-wisdom): a whole blog in a book!

## Podcasts

- [RTFM](https://anchor.fm/aaron-king2/): Every episode gives me something to think about.
- [Between Two Cairns](https://betweentwocairns.com/) OSR stuff. Great outros.
- [Yes Indie'd](https://www.podchaser.com/podcasts/yes-indied-pod-997310) insightful interviews.
- [Blogs on Tape](https://blogsontape.paperspencils.com/) I don't like reading blogs on my computer, so I listen to them or read them in print whenever I can!
- [We Read the Bloggies](https://podcast.bloggies.org/@WeReadTheBloggies) more blogs you can listen to!
- [Barbell Medicine](https://www.barbellmedicine.com/podcast/) is my go to for gym content.
- [Deep Questions](https://www.thedeeplife.com/listen/) is self-help that has helped me.

## Misc

- [FWD RPG PNW](https://fwdrpg.org/): Runs free, beginner friendly RPGs in Portland!
- [Go Play NW](https://goplaynw.org/): A lovely indie TTRPG con in Seattle.
- [Dropout.tv](https://www.dropout.tv/): More satisfying than YouTube.
- [The Games in "Children's Games"](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Children's_Games_(Bruegel)#The_games) is my favorite list.
